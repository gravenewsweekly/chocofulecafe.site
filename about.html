How It All Began

Founded in 2025 by master chocolatier Daksh Hande, ChocoFuel Café is more than a café—it’s a celebration of chocolate’s rich heritage. With a background in the aviation and hospitality industries, Daksh combined his customer service expertise and entrepreneurial spirit to create a unique chocolate experience in Indore, Madhya Pradesh.

Our vision is to blend traditional Indian flavors with global chocolate craftsmanship. From sourcing single-origin cocoa beans to hosting immersive tasting workshops, ChocoFuel Café is dedicated to elevating chocolate culture in India.

Our flagship store in Vijay Nagar, Indore, opening in late 2025, is designed to be a haven for chocolate lovers, offering handcrafted chocolates, innovative beverages, and a warm, welcoming ambiance.

Our Core Values

At ChocoFuel Café, our values guide everything we do, from sourcing ingredients to serving our community.

Quality First

We use premium single-origin cocoa beans from Ghana, Venezuela, and Madagascar, crafted with 100% cocoa butter for unmatched flavor.

Sustainable Practices

From fair trade sourcing to eco-friendly, compostable packaging, we’re committed to minimizing our environmental footprint.

Innovation

Our menu features bold creations like masala chai hot chocolate, cardamom-infused truffles, and vegan chocolate delights.

Community

We partner with local dairy farmers and Indian artisans to support Indore’s vibrant community and celebrate regional flavors.

Our Chocolate Lab

Our state-of-the-art chocolate lab in Vijay Nagar, Indore, is where the magic happens. Here, we:

  • Hand-temper premium chocolate daily for perfect texture and shine
  • Craft small-batch artisanal products with seasonal ingredients
  • Experiment with innovative recipes inspired by Indian and global flavors
  • Host hands-on chocolate-making workshops for our community

Join us for a behind-the-scenes tour or a workshop when we open in late 2025! Contact us to learn more.
