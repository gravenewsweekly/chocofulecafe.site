How It All Began

Founded in 2025 by master chocolatier Daksh Hande, ChocoFuel Café was born from a simple idea: to create a space where chocolate isn't just a treat, but an experience.

After spending few months apprenticing with previous business venture experience and customer service experience , Daksh came up with a mission to elevate chocolate culture in Indore.

Our flagship store in Vijay Nagar is the realization of this dream - a place where traditional Indian techniques meet Indian flavors.
