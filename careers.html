How to Apply

Join our team and help shape Indore’s premier chocolate destination! Follow these steps to apply for any of our open positions:

1. Visit Our Contact Page

Go to our Contact page and select “Job Application” in the form’s subject dropdown.

2. Specify the Job Title

Clearly mention the position you’re applying for in your message (e.g., “Chocolatier” or “Café Manager”).

3. Submit Your Resume

Attach your resume and, if applicable, a portfolio link or relevant work samples. If you can’t attach files, email them to careers@chocofuelcafe.site.

Note: Only shortlisted candidates will be contacted for interviews within two weeks. All positions are full-time and based in Vijay Nagar, Indore. We are an equal opportunity employer and value diversity.
