Our Story

Founded in 2025 by passionate chocolatier Daksh Hande, ChocoFuel Café is more than just a café—it’s a celebration of chocolate’s rich heritage and bold flavors. With a background in customer service and a lifelong love for chocolate, Daksh envisioned a space where indulgence meets innovation. Located in Indore, Madhya Pradesh, ChocoFuel Café is set to redefine how you experience chocolate.

Our mission is to craft unforgettable moments through ethically sourced, handcrafted chocolates and beverages. From single-origin cocoa to innovative chocolate-infused coffee, every creation at ChocoFuel is designed to fuel your senses and spark joy.

Grand Opening Details

Join us for our grand opening in late 2025 at our flagship location in Vijay Nagar, Indore. Our café will offer:

  • Handcrafted artisanal chocolates made with premium ingredients
  • Signature chocolate-infused coffee and beverages
  • Interactive chocolate tasting workshops and pairing events
  • Customizable chocolate gift boxes for every occasion
  • Cozy ambiance perfect for chocolate lovers and coffee enthusiasts
